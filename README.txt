== Description ==

Send your marketing SMS via your smartphone.

Low-cost SMS marketing through your mobile plan :
text your customers from your e-store, 
the SMS are sent by your smartphone.

== Required ==
install Ardary inSIM app from the Play Store on your android phone, 
it turns your smartphone into a SMS server connected to your WooCommerce.

== Features ==

All-in-one SMS solutions of large companies for your small business :
- SMS campaigns (list creator, analytics, tracking on clicks)
- SMS automations (shipping confirmation, order status update, new customer welcome message, ...)
- SMS helpdesk CRM (answer your customer trough their favorite channel, the SMS)

inSIM for WooCommerce details : [https://ardary-insim.com/woocommerce/]

== Installation ==

This section describes how to install the plugin and get it working.

e.g. 

1. Upload `inSIM.zip` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Unzip `inSIM.zip`
3. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress


== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Do I need an account ? =

Yes, you need an ardary-sms account to send sms and get full analytics and details. You can create an account on [ardary-sms.com/solo].
